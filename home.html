Magic! The Gathering

Primarily an Commander player, I play a lot of heads-up with my partner or do some 40-card drafting. On casual nights, you can sometimes find us out at Odyssey for some multiplayer (though we are always up for a kitchen table game). My primary deck is Inalla, archmage ritualist (blue, black, red, wizards-tribal reanimation) though if you're up for some meme-Brawl, I have a Niv-Mizzut Parun deck called Dominizzit that is hilariously fun to play. I keep trying to get into Arena, especially given the pandemic, but my print collection is too extensive to abandon easily... If you're ever looking for a game, check out my about me section to send me an email!

Board Sports

Snowboarding and wakeboarding are my foremost boardsport loves, though lately Kiteboarding has been a growing passion. Bittersweet, Timber Ridge and Crystal Mountain are my typical winter "local" spots, though Whistler-Blackcomb in Vancouver forever has my heart. In the summer, Holland has some nice spots for wakeboarding, surfing and kiteboarding, though nothing will beat the Outer Banks, NC.

Books

Epic sci-fi and fantasy all the way. A few all-time favorite authors include: Patrick Rothfuss, Robert Jordan and Brandon Sanderson. I am always looking for something new to read. (send me a recommendation)
